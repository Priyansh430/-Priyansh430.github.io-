## Profile
I am an undergraduate mathematics student at IISER Bhopal with research interests in algebraic complexity theory, proof complexity, and computational complexity theory. My current work focuses on problems in algebraic graph theory and the complexity of matrix invariants, with particular emphasis on arithmetic circuits, the classes VP and VNP, and determinant complexity. I am interested in understanding the interplay between algebraic structures and computational complexity.
